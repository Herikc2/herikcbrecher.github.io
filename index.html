Machine Learning

Personalized Medicine - Redefining the Treatment of Cancer

In recent years, there has been a lot of talk about how precision medicine, and more specifically genetic testing, will revolutionize the treatment of diseases like cancer.
However, this transformation has been partially slowed down by the enormous amount of manual work that is still required. In this project, we aim to fully realize the potential of personalized medicine. A single time-sequenced carcinogen tumor can have thousands of genetic mutations, and the challenge is to identify the mutations that contribute to tumor growth.
Currently, this interpretation of mutations is being done manually by clinical pathologists who have to review and classify each genetic mutation based on evidence from the literature and text-based clinical reports. This is a difficult and time-consuming task.
